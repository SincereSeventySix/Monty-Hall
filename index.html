Monty Hall Game Test

The Monty Hall Game is a game where a user is presented three doors. One of the doors contains a prize and the user has to guess which one. After their guess, the user is shown what's behind one of the doors and has the option to either stay with their choice or switch their initial choice. This program runs the test as many times as you want to decipher wheather staying or switching is a better strategy for winning the prize.

1) Choose weather to stay or switch.

2) Enter number of times you want the test to run.
